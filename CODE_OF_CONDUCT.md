# Code of conduct

Amphibia contributors must communicate respectfully and in good faith. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, and sustained personal attacks are not acceptable in project spaces.

Focus review on observable behavior, code, tests, security, licensing, and user impact. Respect creators, upstream maintainers, service providers, and users. Do not pressure anyone to disclose private information or credentials.

Maintainers may edit or remove harmful contributions and may restrict participation when needed to protect the community. Until a canonical project contact exists, moderation concerns should be raised privately with the repository owner rather than posted with sensitive details.
